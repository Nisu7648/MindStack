# ğŸ“Š **ACCOUNTING SYSTEM (IMA RULES)**

## ğŸ¯ **Institute of Management Accountants (IMA) Standards**

### **1. DOUBLE-ENTRY BOOKKEEPING**

**Fundamental Principle:**
```
Every transaction has TWO sides:
- Debit (Dr.) - Left side
- Credit (Cr.) - Right side

Total Debits = Total Credits (ALWAYS!)
```

**The Golden Rules:**

#### **Personal Accounts (People/Organizations):**
```
Debit:   The Receiver
Credit:  The Giver

Example:
Customer ABC Ltd pays 5,000 CHF
Dr. Cash A/c           5,000
    Cr. ABC Ltd A/c           5,000
```

#### **Real Accounts (Assets):**
```
Debit:   What Comes In
Credit:  What Goes Out

Example:
Bought furniture for 2,000 CHF cash
Dr. Furniture A/c      2,000
    Cr. Cash A/c              2,000
```

#### **Nominal Accounts (Income/Expenses):**
```
Debit:   All Expenses and Losses
Credit:  All Incomes and Gains

Example:
Paid rent 1,500 CHF
Dr. Rent Expense A/c   1,500
    Cr. Cash A/c              1,500
```

### **2. ACCOUNTING EQUATION**

```
Assets = Liabilities + Equity

Or:

Assets = Liabilities + Capital + Revenue - Expenses
```

**This MUST balance at all times!**

### **12. DEPRECIATION**

#### **Methods:**

**Straight Line Method:**
```
Annual Depreciation = (Cost - Salvage Value) / Useful Life

Example:
Equipment Cost: 10,000 CHF
Salvage Value: 1,000 CHF
Useful Life: 5 years

Annual Depreciation = (10,000 - 1,000) / 5 = 1,800 CHF/year
```

**Reducing Balance Method:**
```
Annual Depreciation = Book Value Ã— Rate%

Example:
Equipment Cost: 10,000 CHF
Rate: 20%

Year 1: 10,000 Ã— 20% = 2,000 CHF
Year 2: 8,000 Ã— 20% = 1,600 CHF
Year 3: 6,400 Ã— 20% = 1,280 CHF
```

### **13. INVENTORY VALUATION**

#### **Methods:**

**FIFO (First In, First Out):**
```
Purchases:
Jan 5:  100 units @ 10 CHF = 1,000 CHF
Jan 15: 100 units @ 12 CHF = 1,200 CHF

Sales:
Jan 20: 150 units

Cost of Goods Sold:
100 units @ 10 CHF = 1,000 CHF
50 units @ 12 CHF = 600 CHF
Total COGS = 1,600 CHF

Closing Inventory:
50 units @ 12 CHF = 600 CHF
```

**LIFO (Last In, First Out):**
```
Same purchases and sales as above

Cost of Goods Sold:
100 units @ 12 CHF = 1,200 CHF
50 units @ 10 CHF = 500 CHF
Total COGS = 1,700 CHF

Closing Inventory:
50 units @ 10 CHF = 500 CHF
```

**Weighted Average:**
```
Same purchases as above

Average Cost = (1,000 + 1,200) / 200 = 11 CHF per unit

Cost of Goods Sold:
150 units @ 11 CHF = 1,650 CHF

Closing Inventory:
50 units @ 11 CHF = 550 CHF
```
